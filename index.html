About

The Rangelands Literature Map of Arizona is a pilot project developed by Andrew Antaya, Cody Wooden, and Jeanne Pfander from the University of Arizona. This map uses open source software to geo-reference/geo-tag journal articles from the Journal of Range Management/Rangeland Ecology & Management, and display the article citations on an interative web map. The goal of this project is to demonstrate the feasibility of adding geographic coordinates to journal articles, with the intent of revolutionizing literature searches away from keywords and towards place-based searches.
